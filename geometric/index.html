Sierpinski's Triangle (standard rules)
Square1 Fractal (not same verticy twice)
Square2 Fractal (not verticy clockwise or counterclockwise of previous)
Square3 Fractal (not verticy 2 away from previous)
Square4 Fractal (if two previous verticies are the same, cannot neighbor them)
Pentagon1 Fractal (not the same verticy twice)
Pentagon2 Fractal (if two previous verticies are the same, cannot neighbor them)
Hexagon1 Fractal (not the same verticy twice)
